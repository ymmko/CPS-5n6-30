Lab 02

The subject for this webage will be the requirements for Lab 02.

  1. The page subject is entirely yours
  2. Your name and student number must appear clearly on the main page
  3. You must have at least one image. The image in this case is the background.
  4. You must have a table that uses at least one colspan or rowspan attribute.
  5. You must use at least two HTML5-only features.
  6. You must use CSS.
  7. You must use at least two CSS3-only features.
  8. You must define at least one element class or id in your style sheet (ex. div.myclass or p#myid).
  9. You must define one global style class or id in your style sheet (ex. .myclass or #myid)
  10. You must validate your web page for HTML5 compliancy and CSS3 compliancy and save the report into a file.
  11. Your web page must have a link to your validation report and technical report.
  12. You must host your page on a server of your choice.
  13. The access to your page can be password protected (not mandatory). You use a method of your choosing to do it. It can be the same one as lab #1.
  14. Write a technical report in HTML that contains all the following elements
    • What is your element CSS class?
    • What is your global CSS class?
    • What are your HTML5-only tags?
    • What are your CSS3-only features?
    • Did you use the same protection method? If no, what did you use this time?
    • How long did you spend on this lab (approximately)
